Current Events

Vietnam's economy ranks 37th in the world (by GDP). Much of their economy is based on agriculture. They have the 5th largest export of rice in the world (#1 being Bangladesh). Tourism is another large money-maker for Vietnam as yearly they bring in millions of tourists, unfortunately during the COVID-19 pandemic the country lost a lot of revenue (as did most countries) due to global efforts to slow the spread of the pandemic. The currency used in Vietnam is the Vietnamese Dong.
The dominant politcal party in Vietnam is the communist party. The country is divided into 64 provinces which can further be divided into urban districts and rural districts. At the lowest level of administration there are around ten thousand communes. At all of these levels the People's Council holds the governmental power which they gain through election.
