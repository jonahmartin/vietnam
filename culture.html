Vietnam has a rich history of music, from traditional vietnamese music used for religious purposes/festivals to modern day V-Pop with artists such as Son Tung M-TP, My Tam, and Lam Truong. There are a range of fascinating musical instruments that have been used in traditional Vientamese music for centuries such as the dan bau which is a one stringed instument which has a large range of sounds it can produce.

The list of delicious cuisine from Vietnam is extensive. Their food makes use of many ingredients & spices such as lemongrass, ginger, mint, coriander, lime, and basil. Much of their food makes use of fresh vegetables, and very little in the way of dairy. The most commonly used meat in traditional Vietnamese recipes is pork. For anyone who loves food, Vietnamese food is a treasure-trove of delicious dishes that are sure to delight anyone.
